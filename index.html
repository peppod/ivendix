About PepPod

PepPod was Imagined by an avid backpacker exploring the far corners of the world for long periods at a time who learned water was fairly easy to find but could not find the nutrition and energy needed.

Envisioned abroad, created in Colorado, and produced in the U.S.A.

Drink a Pod

before you the the trail to empower you body and mind.

Throw a couple Pods

in a large water bottle to maintain your performance throughout the day.

At a mere 3.7 grams

PepPod is highly portable.

Our ideal combination of vitamins

over 75 plant-derived trace minerals, electrolytes, amino acids, antioxidants, and ginseng.

80mg of caffeine

(less than a cup of coffee) provides a boost you can feel great about.

Perfectly sized

tablet fits easily into any water bottle or glass and dissolves completely.
