PepPod is for you

You pursue infinite possibilities and limitless passions while managing to tackle your daily responsibilities. That's no small feat and providing your mind and body with the optimal nutrition isn’t always easy or convenient.

PepPod is here to help.

We’ve created a convenient and healthy nutritional energy tablet designed for daily use that optimizes human performance and empowers you to do what you love. From weekend warriors to professional athletes, business professionals to moms on the go.

Envisioned abroad, created in Colorado, and produced in the U.S.A.

Drink a Pod

...before you the the trail to empower you body and mind.

Throw a couple Pods

...in a large water bottle to maintain your performance throughout the day.

At a mere 3.7 grams

...PepPod is highly portable.

Our ideal combination of vitamins

..over 75 plant-derived trace minerals, electrolytes, amino acids, antioxidants, and ginseng.

80mg of caffeine

...(less than a cup of coffee) provides a boost you can feel great about.

Perfectly sized

...tablet fits easily into any water bottle or glass and dissolves completely.

Healthy and natural

...GMO-free, low-calorie, vegetarian, sugar-free, dairy-free, gluten-free, and diabetic-friendly.
